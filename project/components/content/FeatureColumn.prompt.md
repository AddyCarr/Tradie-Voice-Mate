The signature "Large Number Columns" layout — an oversized Deep Teal numeral over a headline and short body, with a left hairline divider.

```jsx
<FeatureColumn number="01" title="24/7 coverage">
  On site, in the ute, or 10pm Sunday — every call answered.
</FeatureColumn>
```

Lay out 3-across (2 rows for 6 features). Defaults to dark sections; pass `onDark={false}` on light. Set `divider={false}` on the first column of a row if you don't want a leading rule.
